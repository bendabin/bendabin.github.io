About Me

Hi! My name is Ben and I enjoy building my own websites and website applications. I have a strong passion for science and technology and building things that improve the user's experience.

I have been living in Japan since 2020. I first came to Japan on a one year working holiday. I fell in love with Japanese culture and history and have decided to make Japan my permanent home.

I specialize in Full-Stack web development using a combination of Front-End programming languages such as HTML5 and CSS3 and Back-End programming languages such as Python, Javascript and PHP. I also know other programming languages such as Java, C# and many more.
